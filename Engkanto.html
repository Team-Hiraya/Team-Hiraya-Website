Engkanto: The Hidden Realm

Engkanto (from Spanish encanto, lit. “enchantment”) are mythical environmental spirits that can appear in human form. They are often associated with ancestral spirits called anito in the Philippines.

Introduction

Engkanto is a 2D action-adventure game inspired by Hollow Knight and Silksong, reimagined in a Filipino setting. Set in a dark, mysterious world filled with creatures from Philippine mythology, the game blends exploration, combat, and storytelling.

You play as Laurel, a young girl armed with arnis, who must journey across diverse regions, uncover secrets, and find a way back to the real world.
